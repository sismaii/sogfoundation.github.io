Let Us Overcome The Pandemic Together!


Our Foundation is non-profitable, that is designing interventions to help hospitals and patients with oxygen, food, and health support. Help them save thousands of lives by donating to this cause. This is a platform that enables those in need to reach out to organizations or volunteers that are offering free meal services to people affected by Covid and their families. We are distributing ration kits and food packets among poor and needy patients.
